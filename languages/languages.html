My Languages!

Below, I wanted to highlight the two languages that I speak natively.

English
Having been born in the U.S. and having received English-speaking education since preschool, I now proficiently utilize English at a native-speaker level.
Chinese (Mandarin)
Although I was born in the U.S., my Chinese-speaking family and I moved to Beijing, China when I was five years old. I resided in Beijing for 10 years, during which I acquired fluency in Mandarin at a native-speaker level. I continue to utilize Mandarin in both my professional and social settings on a daily basis.
